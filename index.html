How to Clean Wetpour Surfaces?

Cleaning wetpour surfaces requires a systematic approach to remove debris, moss, and algae thoroughly. A pressure washer with a suitable surface cleaner attachment can effectively clean and rejuvenate rubber surfaces in playgrounds.event potential future issues, ultimately extending the lifespan of the Wet Pour surface and maintaining its safety standards.

  1. Start by preparing the pressure washer with the appropriate nozzle and detergent for cleaning rubber surfaces.
  2. Begin by spraying the surface with water to loosen any dirt or debris.
  3. Next, apply the detergent solution to break down the moss and algae growth. Allow the detergent to sit for a few minutes to penetrate the surface.

Then, using the pressure washer, work in sections to blast away the loosened debris, moss, and algae. Be sure to maintain a safe distance to prevent damaging the rubber surface.

Move methodically across the wetpour area, ensuring all areas are thoroughly cleaned.

Once the cleaning process is complete, rinse the surface with clean water to remove any remaining detergent residue. Allow the wetpour surface to dry completely before allowing any foot traffic.

Regular maintenance and cleaning of wetpour surfaces are essential to preserve their longevity and ensure the safety of playground users.
